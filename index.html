About GDG Wah Campus

Connect. Learn. Grow.

Google Developer Group Wah Campus is an initiative to concentrate the efforts of many developers in and around Wah Cantt towards learning, sharing, and developing with Google technologies.

Our goal is to create a vibrant local tech community, foster learning, and provide a platform for students to network with peers and industry experts. We organize hands-on workshops, tech talks, and hackathons focusing on a wide range of topics from Android and Web development to Cloud and AI.
